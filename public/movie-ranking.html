The Best TV Shows According to Kenzie

My top 3 shows of all-time.

Riverdale

Riverdale is my favourite TV show because it mixes mystery, drama and unexpected twists in a way that keeps me hooked every episode. I love how characters, like Betty Cooper and Jughead Jones, all havecomplex personalities and storylines that constantly evolve. It's not just a typical teen show, it's dramatic, unpredictable, and always leaves you wanting more, which is exactly why I enjoyed it so much.

The Vampire Diaries

The Vampire Diaries is one of my favourite shows because it perfectly blends romance, drama,a nd supernatural tension in a way thats impossible to stop watching. I love how characters like Elena Gilbert and Damon Salvatore have such intense relationships and emotional storylines that keeps evolving. It's the mix of love, danger, and constant twists that makes it so addictive and the reason I enjoy it so much.

Euphoria

Euphoria is one of my favourite shows because it's raw, emotional, and visually different from anything else I've watched. I like how charcters like Rue Bennett and Maddy Perez feel real and complex, with stroylines that explore friendships, relationships, and growing up in an intense way. It's the mix of powerful emotions, aesthetic visuals, and unpredictable moments that makes it so captivating to me.
